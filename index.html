Greetings. Please step into my e-office.

My name is Ross Collier and I am an audio engineer & omnichordist in Nashville, TN. I am very, very serious. It is a pleasure to e-meet you.

If you are looking for audio work, you are in the right place. If you are looking for Omnichords, please click here!

If you are hoping to do a project, you can contact me via e-mail (see link below). If you are very hip, you can find me via social media and that will be fine too.

My day rate is $250. If you have smaller projects I charge hourly at $35. For mastering work I charge $40 for singles; for EPs or full albums I like to work with clients to find a price for all of the songs. I am registered with the RIAA to assign ISRC codes to your songs at the mastering stage.
